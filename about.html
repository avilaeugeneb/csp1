History

A socio-civic organization that started as bird-watching group in 1987. First recognized as a student environmental socio-civic organization in 1989.

The organization envisions and promote sustainable development in the Philippines. The organization advocates the need for the management of the natural resources to the community-based, socially equitable, and scientifically sound. Haring Ibon UPLB holds activities that aim to promote environmental awareness in and out of the university.

Mission

The organization seeks to actively work and contribute to sustainable development by raising the national consciousness amongst Filipinos.

Vision

Haring Ibon UPLB's vision is sustainable development for the Philippines. This means creatively ensuring that "the needs of the present are met without compromising the ability of the future generations to meet their own needs". (from the World Commission on Environment and Development)

Objectives

The HARING-IBON UPLB shall contribute to a national development that is sustainable and socially just. It shall be the organizational vehicle through which the students of the UPLB community can participate in the pursuance of the following:

  1. Maintenance and/or restoration of ecological balance and a healthy environment
  2. Conservation and sustainable development of natural resources
  3. Promotion of social equity in the use of natural resources
  4. Preservation of critical areas and endangered species of flora and fauna
  5. Management of protected areas and wildlife
  6. Management of waste and pollutants
  7. Preservation of ethnic Filipino culture
  8. Protection of people's right to a healthful ecology
  9. Promotion of favorable change in values and attitude towards nature
